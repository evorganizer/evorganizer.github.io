1. Who this applies to

This policy covers everyone who uses EvOrganizer: event organizers, service providers, insurers, and banks (together, "members"). Because EvOrganizer connects these four kinds of members to plan and pay for events, some information you share is necessarily visible to other members — this policy explains exactly what, and to whom.

2. What we collect

  • Account information — name, contact details, and the role you sign up as (organizer, provider, insurer, or bank).
  • Event details — what organizers create when planning an event: dates, vendors booked, budget, and guest-related logistics needed to run it.
  • Provider listings — services, pricing, availability, and credentials that providers choose to publish.
  • Payment and financial data — transaction records needed to process bookings, insurance, and financing. Card and bank details are handled by our payment and banking partners, not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data — device, log, and app-interaction data that helps us keep EvOrganizer reliable and secure.

3. How information moves between members

EvOrganizer's purpose is to connect roles that used to coordinate by phone and paper. That means:

  • Organizers see provider listings, availability, and public reviews.
  • Providers see the event details an organizer shares when requesting a booking.
  • Insurers see the event information needed to quote and issue coverage an organizer requests.
  • Banks see the transaction information needed to process a payment or financing request an organizer or provider initiates.

No member sees more than what's needed to fulfil the specific request they're part of.

4. Why we process this information

To operate your account, connect you with the right members, process payments and contracts securely, meet our legal and regulatory obligations (including those that apply to our insurer and bank partners), and improve the reliability of the app.

5. Where information is stored and applicable law

EvOrganizer is built for organizers and members across Ghana and the wider region. We handle personal data in line with Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), and where members are located elsewhere, the data protection law that applies to them.

6. Data retention

We keep account and event data for as long as your account is active, plus whatever period is needed to satisfy financial, insurance, or tax record-keeping obligations after that. You can request deletion of your account; some records may be retained where the law requires it.

7. Your choices

  • Access, correct, or export the personal data tied to your account.
  • Request deletion of your account, subject to the retention rules above.
  • Control what a provider listing or organizer profile makes public to other members.
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications at any time.

8. Children's privacy

EvOrganizer is intended for adults planning, providing, insuring, or financing events, and is not directed at children.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a way that affects how your information is used, we'll let members know before the change takes effect.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to privacy.evorganizer@outlook.com.
